About Me

Hello! I'm Bixi Huang, a passionate individual at the intersection of healthcare and technology. My journey has taken me from the halls of The College of New Jersey, where I pursued nursing, to the dynamic world of computer science and artificial intelligence. I thrive on challenges and believe in the power of adaptability and continuous learning.

Background

Originally an aspiring nurse, I navigated the complexities of human anatomy and patient care with curiosity and dedication. However, facing financial and linguistic hurdles as an international student, I learned early on the importance of resilience. A transformative experience in my family's machinery company in China opened my eyes to the fascinating world of technology. Here, I discovered my true calling in the digital realm, particularly in artificial intelligence and web development.

Philosophy

I believe that every challenge is an opportunity for growth. My diverse experiences have taught me to look beyond the conventional and embrace the potential of technology to transform lives. With a unique blend of healthcare knowledge and tech expertise, I am excited to explore innovative solutions in the tech industry.
